# Research

Our research in the SHARP Lab focuses on bridging mechanistic toxicology across species and biological scales.

## 1. Comparative Toxicogenomics
We integrate transcriptomics, pathway analysis, and evolutionary conservation to compare hazard responses across model organisms.

## 2. High-throughput Behavioural Toxicology
Using automated phenotyping in *Daphnia* and *Drosophila*, we quantify real-time behavioural fingerprints of chemical exposure.

## 3. Mechanism-informed NAMs
We develop AOP and network-based approaches to connect molecular perturbations with organism-level adverse outcomes.

## 4. One Health & Environmental Risk
Our work links human toxicology and ecotoxicology under climate-relevant environmental stressors.
