# Join the SHARP Lab

We are always interested in hearing from motivated students and researchers.

---

## PhD Opportunities
Potential projects include:

- Daphnia molting-associated behaviour and molecular assessment of environmental chemicals
- Comparative toxicology
- Omics2AOP and qAOP modelling
- Climate-informed One Health ecotoxicology
- Liver-thyroid axis organ-on-chip for assessment of endocrine disruption chemicals

Applicants with backgrounds in toxicology, bioinformatics, systems biology, environmental science, or computational modelling are particularly welcome.

---

## Visiting Students and Exchanges
We welcome visiting PhD students, interns, and exchange researchers, especially through:

- University of Birmingham–Nanjing University Joint Centre
- PARC / PrecisionTox collaborations
- Erasmus and international exchange schemes
